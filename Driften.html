What are drifting servers?

Drifting servers are basicly racing servers if you'd ask me. The only, and most importand, difference between those two is the width and form of the tracks. You will find yourself on a track thats way wider and has way more turns when you join a drifting server whilst regular race servers also have tracks with small paths and long straight parts.
